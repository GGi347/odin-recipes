Paratha

Description

Parathas are traditional Tibetan steamed dumplings. Very easy to make and disappear quickly!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Onions
  • Potato
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. knead the doh.
  2. mash the boiled potatoes
  3. Make balls of doh with potatoes inside
  4. Make flatbreads and apply the necessary oil.
